About Antares Project

A new star is born, and is growing fast.
Antares is an SQL client that aims to become an useful and complete tool, especially for developers.
The target is to support as many databases as possible, and all major operating systems, including the ARM versions.
At the moment this application is in development state, many features will come in future updates, and actually supports MySQL/MariaDB, PostgreSQL and SQLite.
Most of its current features are enough to have a pleasant user experience with MySQL/MariaDB, PostgreSQL and SQLite, so give it a chance and send us your feedback, we would really appreciate it.

Simple, but complete
